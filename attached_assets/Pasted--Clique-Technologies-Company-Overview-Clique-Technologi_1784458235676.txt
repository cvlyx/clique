# Clique Technologies

## Company Overview

**Clique Technologies** is a forward-thinking technology company focused on developing innovative software solutions and delivering reliable digital services that empower businesses, organizations, and individuals in the modern digital economy.

Founded by **Calyx Chisiza (Founder & CEO)**, Clique Technologies was established with a vision of becoming a leading technology company that creates impactful solutions through software engineering, artificial intelligence, data science, and digital transformation.

We specialize in designing, developing, and implementing scalable technology solutions that solve real-world problems. From custom software applications and mobile platforms to artificial intelligence systems, data-driven solutions, cybersecurity, and IT infrastructure, Clique Technologies helps organizations leverage technology to improve efficiency, productivity, and growth.

Our goal is not only to provide technology services but also to build innovative products that shape the future of how people and businesses interact with technology.
