CVLYX

CVLYX (Calyx Nthwachi Chisiza) is a talented artist hailing from Mzuzu, Malawi. His musical journey began at the young age of 12, when he discovered his passion for hip-hop, afro, and R&B music. CVLYX's unique blend of genres allows him to create captivating and soulful tracks that resonate with listeners. With his real name as a foundation, he weaves stories and emotions into his music, leaving an indelible mark on the Malawian music scene.
